## 1. Definition
Data privacy refers to the protection of personal and sensitive information
from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

## 2. Types of Data
- Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
- Financial data
- Health records
- Corporate confidential data

## 3. Legal Framework
- GDPR (EU)
- LOPDGDD (Spain)
- HIPAA (Healthcare)

## 4. Main Risks
- Data breaches
- Phishing attacks
- Insider threats
- Misconfigured systems

## 5. Security Controls
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular backups
- Security awareness training

## 6. CIA Triad
- Confidentiality: Prevent unauthorized access
- Integrity: Prevent unauthorized modification
- Availability: Ensure data access when needed

## 7. Professional Reflection
Understanding data privacy is essential to maintain trust,
ensure compliance, and reduce organizational risk.
